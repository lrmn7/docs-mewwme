After [inviting Mewwme into your server](/adding-mewwme), you can get the party started! To begin playing songs through Mewwme, follow this guide:

## 1. Join a Voice Channel.

- You can do that by clicking on the channel with a speaker icon.

![Joining a Voice Channel](/img/docs/playing-a-song/joinVC.png)

## 2. Summon Mewwme into the Voice Channel

- You can make Mewwme join the Voice Channel by typing `me join`.
  ![Joining a Voice Channel](/img/docs/playing-a-song/acousticjoin.png)

## 3. Start your music.

- Use the command `me play` to start your music.

:::caution When playing a playlist:

- Make sure the playlist is either `Unlisted` or `Public`. If it is set to `Private`, Acoustic won't be able to play that specific playlist.
:::

## 4. Search for more results

- If you need more search results, you can use `me searchsong <song name>` and choose the option you want.
